What is the Occult Archive?

The Occult Archive is a non-profit, community driven site dedicated towards archiving and long-term preservation of any and all esoteric and occult content.  We curate, maintain and organize occult media ranging from videos, books to audio files. Currently, we host over 1.5TB of Occult content that is made available to any Occult Archive member. 

All of our content can be downloaded by members and is maintained by Occult Archive librarians.

Our Mission

 

Through knowledge freedom and power can be gained, we can enlighten ourselves and improve our realities and those realities around us.  Through eons of time knowledge has been written and lost, burned and forgotten. In this recent digital age it is our duty as technologists and occultists to do what we can to preserve the esoteric knowledge of the world for future learners.  Our mission is to collect and preserve

all esoteric knowledge

 

The Occult Archive was born out of the need to aggregate and collect Occult media to make available to interested students of the Occult.
